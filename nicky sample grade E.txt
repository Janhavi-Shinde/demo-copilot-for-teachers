What is a World Heritage site?
A world heritage site is a location on the map that has been recognized as a site that is worth acknowledging.

Why is World Heritage Listing important?
Being listed on the world heritage list is a great honour and is a special privilege.

Examples:
Natural and cultural sites should be listed because if they are good enough to be recognized, they are good enough to be listed.
