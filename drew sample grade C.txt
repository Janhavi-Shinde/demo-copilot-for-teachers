A World Heritage Site is a natural or cultural place in the world that is of global significance. It may be because of its beauty, its religious value or its important history. World Heritage listing is important because when a place/site is listed it is a protected place meaning no harm can come to it. It also gives people the opportunity to see the place and experience its great wonders.

Natural and Cultural sites should be World Heritage Listed because of its natural beauty, special or distinctive forms, Architectural Significance (or artistic), Cultural and for religious values.
The Sydney Opera House should be included as a World Heritage site because of its great unique beauty. Also, its amazing eccentric structure is a great highlight. The Sydney Opera House is also of great importance to the Australian people. The Sydney Opera House is a main attraction to tourists from all over the world whilst they are visiting Sydney. It is a lovely place and should definitely be chosen to be on the World Heritage List.
